Ginger Milk Tea (Adrak Chai)

Adrak Chai is a traditional Indian tea made by brewing black tea leaves with fresh ginger (adrak), milk, sugar (optional), and water. The ginger adds a warm, spicy kick that not only tastes amazing but also offers health benefits.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk (usually full-fat, but can be adjusted)
  • Black tea leaves or tea bags (Assam is a popular choice)
  • Fresh ginger (grated or crushed)
  • Sugar (optional, to taste)

How It's Made

  1. Boil water with grated/crushed ginger.
  2. Boil water with grated/crushed ginger.
  3. Add milk and sugar; bring it to a boil again.
  4. Simmer a bit to deepen the flavor.
  5. Strain into cups and enjoy hot.

Health Benefits

  • Soothes sore throat and cold symptoms
  • Aids digestion
  • Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties
  • Gives a cozy caffeine boost

Fun Fact:

In India, chai isn't just a drink — it's a ritual. People bond over chai, start their mornings with it, take chai breaks at work, and even offer it to guests as a warm welcome.
